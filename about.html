Our journey

History of Busia health centre


Post-Independence Expansion (1960s - 1980s) The Ministry of Health embarked on expanding healthcare infrastructure and services to improve access to medical care. More health centers and dispensaries were constructed in Busia to cater to the growing population. Immunization programs and public health campaigns were launched to combat infectious diseases like malaria, tuberculosis, and polio.

Public Health Interventions The department has focused on public health issues such as malaria control, sanitation, and safe water supply. Health campaigns and partnerships with non-governmental organizations have been instrumental in addressing various health challenges.



Leadership

The department of Health Advisory Board.

The Department of Health Advisory Board is a body that provides strategic advice and recommendations to the Department of Health.
Its primary role is to offer expert guidance on public health policies, programs, and practices to improve the overall health outcomes of the population.
